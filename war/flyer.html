Study Name
Utility of tabular structure for nonvisual browsing
Description
The study addresses the question of whether websites presenting data using tables are easier to use with screen readers than websites that use other formats. Participants will be asked to use a series of websites that use different configurations, and their actions will be logged for analysis.
Location
CMU campus, or we can come to locations within the Pittsburgh region.
Eligibility
Participants must be 18 years or older, and should be experienced screen reader users.
